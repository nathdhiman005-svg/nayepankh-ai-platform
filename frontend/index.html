About Us

Think Global,
Act Local

NayePankh Foundation is a non-governmental organisation with a strong desire to help society and make it a better place for all. We are one of the biggest student-led NGOs of India with operations extended in Kanpur, Ghaziabad, and various other cities.

We don't ask for much — just help us with what you can. Be it Money, Skill, or Your Time. Service to mankind is the service to god. Let's revolutionise the society together!
